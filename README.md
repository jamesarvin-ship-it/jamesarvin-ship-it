### 🏗️ What we're building

Mavricon is the **OS for construction** — five product lines, one connected platform:

- **Operations** — Mail · Files · Forms · Issues · Schedule · Site Diary
- **Quality** — Defects · Inspections · Procedures
- **Safety** — Hazards · Permits · SWMS · Toolbox · First Aid
- **Commercials** — Budgets · Contracts · Variations · Costs · Movements
- **Aftercare** — Assets · Handover · Service

Priced on annual construction volume, not user count. AI woven through every tool — not bolted on the side.
